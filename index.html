About Spotlyt

With Spotlyt, users can effortlessly complete quick online tasks directly from their phones and receive payments directly to their South African bank accounts.

Whether it's answering surveys, watching videos, downloading apps, or performing other microtasks, Spotlyt provides a seamless platform for individuals looking to earn extra income on their own terms.

Join Spotlyt today and start monetizing your spare time effortlessly!

How Can I Get Paid?

Sign-Up

Spotlyt's sign-up process is straightforward and user-friendly, allowing new users to create an account quickly and securely. By providing a few key details and verifying their identity, users can gain access to the platform in just a few simple steps.

Complete Tasks

Once signed up, users can dive right into completing a variety of short tasks on their phones, such as engaging with social media content, participating in surveys, or downloading and testing apps and games. These tasks are carefully curated to match users' interests and preferences, making the experience enjoyable and rewarding.

Request Withdrawal

Spotlyt provides users with the flexibility to withdraw their earnings to their bank accounts. With a minimum withdrawal limit of R20 and a maximum limit of R10,000, users have control over how much they want to transfer at any given time.

Get Paid

Once a withdrawal request is made, Spotlyt ensures that users receive their payments promptly and securely to their bank accounts. The platform values transparency and reliability, processing payments efficiently to maintain users' trust.

How Can I Become a Client?

Sign-Up

Spotlyt's sign-up process is straightforward and client-friendly, allowing new clients to create an account quickly and securely. By providing a few key details and verifying their identity, clients can gain access to the client portal in just a few simple steps.

Request Tasks

Once signed up, clients can dive right into requesting a variety of tasks to be completed by our users on their phones, clients can request tasks such as organic social media activity for their social media pages, video and music streams, surveys to be completed by actual people, sign ups to services, and more.

Make Payment

Spotlyt clients have the flexibilty to request tasks at the quantity they require which creates a dynamic price range depending of the task being requested. We accept payments from all South African bank accounts.

Get Your Task Done

Once payment is completed, users will start doing the task requested by the client. All tasks are done in an organic manner by real South Africans.
