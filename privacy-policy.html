Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Spotlyt Task App.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for Spotlyt Task App, we collect information such as your email address, physical address (optional), National Identity Document (NID) number, and user interests.
  • Non-Stored Information: We do not store banking information used for withdrawals. This includes bank name, bank type, and bank account number.

2. Use of Information

  • Email Address: We may use your email address to send you important notifications, updates about our services, and promotional offers.
  • Address: Your physical address, if provided, may be used for location-based services or to verify your eligibility to use our app in South Africa.
  • Identification Number: Your NID number is used for verification purposes to ensure compliance with our eligibility criteria.
  • User Interests: We may use information about your interests to personalise your experience on the app and recommend relevant tasks or promotions.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Stored Information: Personal information collected, such as email address, address, NID number, and user interests, is securely stored on our servers.
  • Data Protection: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Third-Party Services: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate app functionality, such as email communications or analytics. These providers are contractually bound to protect your information and only use it for specified purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose personal information if required to comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from authorities.

5. User Control and Rights

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information through your account settings on Spotlyt Task App.
  • Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions in the emails or contacting us directly.

6. Applicability

Spotlyt Task App operates exclusively in South Africa. Users must provide a South African NID for verification.

7. Policy Updates

  • Consent: By continuing to use Spotlyt Task App after changes to this Privacy Policy, you consent to the updated terms.
